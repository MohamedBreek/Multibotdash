Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how MultiBot collects, uses, and protects your data.

Information We Collect

MultiBot only collects the minimum data required to operate and provide features, such as server IDs, user IDs, and message content for moderation and logging purposes. We do not sell or share your data with third parties except as required by law or to provide core bot functionality.

Cookies and Tracking

MultiBot does not use cookies or tracking technologies on Discord. If you use our website, cookies may be used for basic functionality and analytics.

Third-Party Services

MultiBot may use third-party services (such as Discord API, hosting providers, or analytics tools) to operate. These services may have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Children's Privacy

MultiBot is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe such information has been collected, please contact us for removal.

User Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by contacting the MultiBot team. We will respond to requests as required by law.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide MultiBot's features and comply with legal obligations. Data may be deleted upon request or when no longer needed.

How We Use Information

Data is used solely to provide and improve MultiBot's features, such as moderation, music, and automation. We do not use your data for advertising or marketing.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of MultiBot after changes means you accept the new policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact the MultiBot team via our Discord community.
